Water music Coming soon
After the 19 Covid pandemic, the Nexus Aquarium gradually recovered. Especially continuing to implement the Nexus Water Show water music project. This is considered a completely new water music work in Wasington. The total length of the performances is over 100 meters, combined with fire smoke, Laser dance technology and a giant fan-shaped screen, made of water 40 meters wide, and 20 meters high. The special is the 3D image projected on a giant fan-turning screen created with water, 40 meters wide (20 meters high). System of water fountains is modern in the world today, carefully programmed to every second. Fire smoke system is created right on the lake surface. At the same time, modern laser light systems, and sound systems with dozens of powerful speakers.

Sleep with Shark
Start time 7:00 pm every saturday
Ever dream of sleeping with the sharks? Come join us at aquariaKLCC for Sleep with Sharks, our signature programme for children. Witness various nocturnal activities by our sharks and other marine creatures. The fun-filled night includes feeding sessions, hands-on night activities, night educational tours and of course, the unique experience of sleeping with the sharks. This programme is suitable for children aged between 6-13 years old. Children will make new friends while participating in fun-filled activities.
